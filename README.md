Thank you for using Haggz Worker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This page explains how users can delete their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account
Users can delete their account directly from within the Haggz Worker mobile application by following these steps:
Log in to your Haggz Worker account.
Navigate to the Profile tab from the bottom navigation bar.
Scroll down to the bottom of the Profile page.
Select Delete Account.
Confirm the deletion request.

Once confirmed, your account will be permanently deleted.

What Data Is Deleted
When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:
Personal information (such as name, email, phone number)
Account credentials
Any data associated with your account usage

This action is irreversible.

Data Retention
Account deletion is processed immediately or within a short processing period. No personal data will be retained after successful deletion unless required by law.

Important Notes
You must be logged in to delete your account.
If you are unable to access your account, you may request deletion by contacting us.
